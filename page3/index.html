Michael De Santa

Michael de Santa, born Michael Townley, is a semi-retired bank robber who is forced back into a life of crime at the start of the game. He may be the introductory character, but the jury is still out on whether he edges out the other two playable characters in a popularity contest. Maybe he's just bland enough to get the most votes.

Franklin Clinton

Born sometime in 1988, he went through a rough childhood with an absent father and a drug-addicted mother. At the start of the game, he is trying to make an honest living, but eventually, he joins up with the other two main characters and becomes a bank robber as well.

Trevor Philips

The third and final main playable character in Grand Theft Auto 5 is Trevor Philips. In short, Trevor is a psychopath.
